About The Shanti Niketan School

The global changes have made the world a borderless society. We are faced with challenges that have never been confronted by society at large. We understand the direction of the winds of change and have prepared our educational programmers with that in mind. It is a rare opportunity for the students to be able to go through such a rigorous training and prepare themselves for the world of tomorrow. The Shanti Niketan International School provides its students a conducive environment which is based on cooperative values but is designed to be equally competitive in nature. One of the charms of childhood is that concept of time is unlinked from that of adults. We have, them for a brief moment, and they have us for their entire childhood. We are delighted to have taken the stupendous challenge of nurturing your child, motivating and guiding them to be positive, passionate, focused, confident and holistic personalities. Hence by the time they step into adulthood, they are more than equipped to face the real world and take on the reigns of Indian citizenship with a global outlook.
